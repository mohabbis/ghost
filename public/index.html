Use cases

Built for the chores that live outside your browser.

Ghost is designed for operators, founders, and support teams who repeat the same desktop flows every day but cannot justify a custom integration for each one.

  • Copy customer context between internal tools and native apps.
  • Process invoices, screenshots, forms, and back-office queues.
  • Recreate QA paths without maintaining a brittle script.
  • Delegate repeatable research and admin routines to an AI helper.

Desktop app

Your AI parrot lives in the menu bar.

Ghost runs quietly in your menu bar (macOS) or system tray (Windows), watching for patterns and offering proactive help when it spots repetitive work. Click the parrot to see examples!
